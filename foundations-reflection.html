NEW THINGS I HAVE LEARNT

I learnt that it is hard for me to open up and reach for help.

I found that it is hard to focus on learning when I’m down, but I learnt mindfulness skills to help me focus.

I need to remember we all learn differently and have different strength and limitations, compete only with myself.


THE ROLE OF VALUES, EMPATHY AND SELF-AWARENESS

Those are significant things in learning and programing. It’s important to have empathy to other people and also to ourselves, take ‘mistakes’ as opportunities for growth, and don’t let our ego getting in the way for us to become better programmers.


WHAT HAS SURPRISED ME THE MOST

Definitely the sprint four, neuroplasticity and growth mindset. I was surprised to find out that our mindset can have such a big impact on learning outcomes, while changing my mindset can make the whole learning process more enjoyable and make the critical voices in my head less critical so that I can focus more on learning.


CHALLENGING ASPECT

That would be the first part, introduce myself to the group and share my values and identities.


WHY DO WE SPEND SO MUCH TIME ON CORE LEARNING

Because it’s important! Programmers are problem solvers which means we are spending long hours in solving puzzles and it can be frustrating sometimes, so it is important to have a growth mindset and be kind to ourselves when we face challenge. Another reason is that we work as a team, have empathy and respect for each other is crucial.


CORE LEARNING, A WASTE OF TIME?

No. Through the core learning, I have become more aware of my strengths and limitations. On the other hand, I think not only the study itself is important, but also the ‘not yet’ attitude when I face challenges.
